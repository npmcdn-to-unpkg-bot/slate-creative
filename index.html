You only talk about it when it's bad. It's taken for granted, but we would certainly miss it if it wasn't there.

This is a concept we attempt to integrate into all of our work. We appreciate attention to detail while retaining a practical form. Design should be something functional: something that helps people get through the day and move through their lives. Design serves a noble purpose of conveying ideas to the viewer. The key is allowing design to subtly speak for itself; encouraging the viewer to grasp the concept of his own volition, in a sense not noticing that he was ever persuaded to do so.
